# lane

<generic> portion of road reservation (3.3.1.2) intended to
            accommodate a single line of moving material entities (3.1.1.3) along its length

NOTE: Lanes (3.3.1.12) are often bounded by lane markings.

EXAMPLE: Traffic lane (3.3.1.13), cycle lane (3.3.3.1), sidewalk (3.3.3.4).
