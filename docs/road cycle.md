# road cycle

vehicle (3.7.1.1) meeting the legal requirements to operate in cycle lanes
            (3.3.3.1) and cycleways (3.3.3.2)

NOTE: In some countries, other special lanes (3.3.1.12) could also be
            open to road cycles, such as bus lanes.
