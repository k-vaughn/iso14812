# high vehicle speed

speed (3.7.2.1) of a vehicle (3.7.1.1) that exceeds free flow motorway
            speeds by a factor of two or more

NOTE: Local regulations for the exact speed range vary.

EXAMPLE: A speed in excess of 198 km/h.
