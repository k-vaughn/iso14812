# moderately-low vehicle speed

speed (3.7.2.1) of a vehicle (3.7.1.1) that exceeds speeds typical of pedal
            cycles but can be reached on performance pedal cycles in short bursts or by performance
            riders

NOTE: Local regulations for the exact speed range vary.

EXAMPLE: SAE J3194 associates this with a top speed in the range of 32 km/h to
            48 km/h.
