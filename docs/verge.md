# verge

unpaved part of the roadside (3.3.1.10) designed for travel safety purposes

NOTE: A verge can separate two lanes (3.3.1.12) of different modes,
            separate a traffic lane (3.3.1.13) from a ditch, or separate a lane from another safety
            hazard.
