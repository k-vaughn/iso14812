# low-speed vehicle

vehicle (3.7.1.1) with a design speed (3.7.2.2) in the range of low vehicle
            speeds (3.7.5.8)

NOTE: The term "cycle-speed vehicle" is allowed but the preferred term
            follows a logical progression of levels.

EXAMPLE: Mountain bicycle.
