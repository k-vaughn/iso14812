# low vehicle speed

speed (3.7.2.1) of a vehicle (3.7.1.1) that exceeds pedestrian (3.6.1.4)
            speeds and is more typical of pedal cycles

NOTE: Local regulations for the exact speed range vary.

EXAMPLE: SAE J3194 associates this with a top speed in the range between 13
            km/h and 32 km/h.
