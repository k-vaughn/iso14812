# pick-up/drop-off area

area intended to allow passengers to board and/or alight from vehicles
            (3.7.1.1)

NOTE: The area can include the vehicle waiting area and the pedestrian waiting area.

EXAMPLE: kerbside passenger pick-up area at an airport arrivals zone
