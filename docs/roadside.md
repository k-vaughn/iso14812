# roadside

portion of the road reservation (3.3.1.2) excluding all roadway (3.3.1.3)
            and driving space(s) (3.3.1.4)

NOTE: Roadside, shoulder (3.3.1.18) and verge (3.3.1.11) define
            similar but slightly different concepts
