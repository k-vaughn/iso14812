# jurisdictional entity

person (3.1.1.6) or organization who has jurisdiction (3.4.3.3)

NOTE: In the case of a geopolitical jurisdiction, the jurisdictional
            entity is typically the recognized government for the territory; in the case of a
            campus, the jurisdictional entity is typically the landowner. 

EXAMPLE: A city government
