# concern

<system> interest in a system (3.1.2.1) relevant to one or more of
            its stakeholders (3.1.3.4)

NOTE: A concern pertains to any influence on a system in its
            environment (3.1.3.11), including developmental, technological, business, operational,
            organizational, political, economic, legal, regulatory, ecological and social
            influences.
