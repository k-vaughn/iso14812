# ITS roadside equipment

field system (3.2.1.2) that performs localized ITS services (3.5.3.1)

NOTE: ITS services that can be performed by ITS roadside equipment
            include surveillance, traffic control, information provision, payment transaction
            services, and/or enforcement

EXAMPLE: traffic signal controller, variable message sign, vehicle detection
