# formal coordination

<enterprise view> interaction (3.1.6.4) between two enterprise
            objects (3.1.6.1) governed by a documented agreement

EXAMPLE: A road operator can enter into formal agreement(s) with the owner of a
            road (3.3.5.1) and the owner(s) of the associated roadside (3.3.1.10) equipment.
