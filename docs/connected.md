# connected

characteristic of a material entity (3.1.1.3) that is equipped with enabled
            (i.e. on) and operational electronic communication technologies to support ITS services
            (3.5.3.1), including the means to send and receive data to and from other connected
            entities

NOTE: The term "connected" does not imply support for any specific
            communications interface or ITS-S application process (3.2.9.2).
