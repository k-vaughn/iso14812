# ultra-low vehicle speed

speed (3.7.2.1) of a vehicle (3.7.1.1) that does not greatly exceed typical
            pedestrian (3.6.1.4) speeds

NOTE: Local regulations for the exact speed range vary.

EXAMPLE: SAE J3194 associates this with a top speed of less than 13 km/h.
