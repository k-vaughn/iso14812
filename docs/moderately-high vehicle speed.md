# moderately-high vehicle speed

speed (3.7.2.1) of a vehicle (3.7.1.1) that is typical of free-flow
            motorway speeds to twice that speed

NOTE: Local regulations for the exact speed range vary.

EXAMPLE: A speed in the range of 87 km/h and 198 km/h.
