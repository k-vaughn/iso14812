# moderate vehicle speed

speed (3.7.2.1) of a vehicle (3.7.1.1) that exceeds moderately-low vehicle
            speeds (3.7.5.9) but does not reach free-flow motorway speeds

NOTE: Local regulations for the exact speed range vary.

EXAMPLE: A speed in the range of 48 km/h and 87 km/h.
