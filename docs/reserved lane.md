# reserved lane

traffic lane (3.3.1.13) restricted to a specific subset of road vehicles
            (3.7.6.1) or user (3.5.1.3) categories

NOTE: A traffic lane may be reserved permanently or under certain
            conditions or times.

EXAMPLE: 1 Bus lane.
