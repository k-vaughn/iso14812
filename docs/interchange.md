# interchange

junction (3.3.6.2) with at least one grade separated manoeuvre (3.3.6.10)

NOTE: The term "interchange" typically refers to the facilities that
            enable all available manoeuvres at the grade separated intersection (3.3.6.1), which
            typically includes multiple junctions.
