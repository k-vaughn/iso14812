# concern

<system> interest in a [system](system.md) relevant to one or more of its [stakeholders](stakeholder.md)

<object type="image/svg+xml" data="../../diagrams/concern.dot.svg">
    <img alt="concern Diagram" src="../../diagrams/concern.dot.png" /> <!-- Fallback for non-SVG browsers -->
</object>

Clause: 3.1.3.5

Alternative preferred term: system concern

Note 1 to entry: A concern pertains to any influence on a system in its [environment](environment.md), including developmental, technological, business, operational, organizational, political, economic, legal, regulatory, ecological and social influences.

Source: ISO/IEC/IEEE 42010:2011, 3.7

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
