# road vehicle

[vehicle](vehicle.md) meeting the requirements to operate within the [driving space](driving space.md) of a [road](road.md)

<object type="image/svg+xml" data="../../diagrams/road vehicle.dot.svg">
    <img alt="road vehicle Diagram" src="../../diagrams/road vehicle.dot.png" /> <!-- Fallback for non-SVG browsers -->
</object>

Clause: 3.7.6.1

Note 1 to entry: Unless indicated otherwise, the term typically refers to vehicles that are allowed to operate in the same [driving spaces](driving space.md) as motorized [passenger](passenger.md) cars, according to the legal requirements of the local [jurisdictional entity](jurisdictional entity.md). However, the term can be contextualized for other [environments](environment.md). For example, a bicycle is a [road vehicle](road vehicle.md) when the [road](road.md) is a [cycleway](cycleway.md).

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
