# ITS-S communication unit

physical unit in an [ITS-S unit](ITS-S unit.md) containing part or all of the functionality of the [ITS-S](ITS station.md)

Clause: 3.2.7.5

Alternative preferred term: ITS station communication unit

Alternative preferred term: ITS-SCU

Note 1 to entry: In case an ITS-SU consists of a single physical unit, the ITS-SU and the ITS-SCU are identical. In case an ITS-SU consists of more than one ITS-SCU, then these ITS-SCUs are interconnected via the ITS station-internal network of the ITS-SU.

History note: 2025: Added Note 1 to entry and revised to "the" ITS-S rather than "an" ITS-S.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
