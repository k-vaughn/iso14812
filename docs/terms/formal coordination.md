# formal coordination

<enterprise view> [interaction](interaction.md) between two [enterprise objects](enterprise object.md) governed by a documented agreement

Clause: 3.1.6.5

Alternative preferred term: enterprise view formal coordination

EXAMPLE: A road operator can enter into formal agreement(s) with the owner of a [road](road.md) and the owner(s) of the associated [ITS roadside equipment](ITS roadside equipment.md).
