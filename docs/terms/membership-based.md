# membership-based

characteristic of a [financial model](financial model.md) that requires [users](user.md) to enter into an agreement prior to receiving [services](service.md)

<object type="image/svg+xml" data="../../diagrams/membership-based.dot.svg">
    <img alt="membership-based Diagram" src="../../diagrams/membership-based.dot.png" /> <!-- Fallback for non-SVG browsers -->
</object>

Clause: 3.5.8.8

Note 1 to entry: The agreement can be associated with fees.

Note 2 to entry: The agreement can be minimal, such as collecting user information for business purposes.

Note 3 to entry: The membership can be granted as a part of a broader agreement. For example, a university bus [system](system.md) can restrict access to students and faculty.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
