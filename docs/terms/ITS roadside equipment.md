# ITS roadside equipment

[field system](field system.md) that performs localized [ITS services](ITS service.md)

<object type="image/svg+xml" data="../../diagrams/ITS roadside equipment.dot.svg">
    <img alt="ITS roadside equipment Diagram" src="../../diagrams/ITS roadside equipment.dot.png" /> <!-- Fallback for non-SVG browsers -->
</object>

Clause: 3.2.3.2

Alternative preferred term: ITS roadway equipment

EXAMPLE: traffic signal controller, variable message sign, vehicle detection

Note 1 to entry: "ITS roadway equipment" is used within the current version of the Architecture Reference for Cooperative and Intelligent Transportation (ARC-IT; see Reference [25]). The term [roadway](roadway.md) has been revised to reflect that the equipment is typically along the roadway rather than a part of the roadway.

Note 2 to entry: ITS services that can be performed by ITS roadside equipment include surveillance, traffic control, information provision, payment transaction services, and/or enforcement

Note 3 to entry: While the term "roadside" indicates the typical location of the equipment, ITS roadside equipment (or portions thereof) can be embedded in the roadway itself.

History note: 2025: Added Note 3 to entry.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022.
