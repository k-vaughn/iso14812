# dual carriageway

having exactly two [carriageways](carriageway.md)

Clause: 3.3.1.8

Note 1 to entry: The "dual carriageway" term can be used to describe a [road](road.md), [road section](road section.md), [road link](road link.md), or [road segment](road segment.md).

Note 2 to entry: The two carriageways are typically designed for travel in opposite directions.

History note: 2025: Revised to change term to "dual carriageway" rather than "dual carriageway road segment" to broaden its use and revised definition and note 1 to entry accordingly. Removed admitted term "divided highway" and associated note to entry.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
