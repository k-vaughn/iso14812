# pick-up/drop-off area

area intended to allow passengers to board and/or alight from [vehicles](vehicle.md)

    <img alt="pick-up/drop-off area Diagram" src="../../diagrams/pick-updrop-off area.dot.png" /> <!-- Fallback for non-SVG browsers -->
</object>

Clause: 3.3.8.5

Alternative preferred term: PUDO area

EXAMPLE: kerbside passenger pick-up area at an airport arrivals zone

Note 1 to entry: The area can include the vehicle waiting area and the pedestrian waiting area.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2025
