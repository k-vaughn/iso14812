# pedestrian crossing

physically marked location to enhance the safety of pedestrians crossing one or more lanes of a different mode of travel

<object type="image/svg+xml" data="../../diagrams/pedestrian crossing.dot.svg">
    <img alt="pedestrian crossing Diagram" src="../../diagrams/pedestrian crossing.dot.png" /> <!-- Fallback for non-SVG browsers -->
</object>

Clause: 3.3.3.7

Alternative preferred term: crosswalk

Note 1 to entry: In some cases, local regulations can require vehicles to give way to the pedestrians in the pedestrian crossing and/or restrict pedestrians to only cross at pedestrian crossings.

Note 2 to entry: Pedestrian permission and/or priority at crossing can be managed when traffic signals are present.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2025
