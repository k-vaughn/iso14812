# public

type of [financial model](financial model.md) where [services](service.md) are provided by an administrative authority in an attempt to better serve societal interests

<object type="image/svg+xml" data="../../diagrams/public.dot.svg">
    <img alt="public Diagram" src="../../diagrams/public.dot.png" /> <!-- Fallback for non-SVG browsers -->
</object>

Clause: 3.5.8.3

Note 1 to entry: The transport service [transport service.md] is typically offered to the transport consumer for less than the full capital and operating cost of providing the service, but this is not a requirement.

Note 2 to entry: Typically, the administrative authority is a local government agency; but it could be another administrative authority, such as the national government or a [commercial](commercial.md) [entity](entity.md) managing transport needs [transport needs.md] on their campus.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
