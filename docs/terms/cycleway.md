# cycleway

infrastructure primarily designed for the use of cycles and separate from a [carriageway](carriageway.md)

<object type="image/svg+xml" data="../../diagrams/cycleway.dot.svg">
    <img alt="cycleway Diagram" src="../../diagrams/cycleway.dot.png" /> <!-- Fallback for non-SVG browsers -->
</object>

Clause: 3.3.3.2

Alternative preferred term: cycle track

Note 1 to entry: Local legislation can allow cycleways to be used by a variety of [moderately low-speed vehicles](moderately low-speed vehicle.md) (e.g. low-speed scooters, skateboards, etc.) and can share usage of other low-speed modes such as [pedestrians](pedestrian.md), horseback riders, etc.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
