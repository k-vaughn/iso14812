# loading area

area designated for [vehicles](vehicle.md) to load and/or unload goods

Clause: 3.3.8.2

Alternative preferred term: unloading area

Note 1 to entry: The area can include the vehicle waiting area and the goods waiting area.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2025
