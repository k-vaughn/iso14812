# cycle lane

[lane](lane.md) designed for the through movement of cycles

    <img alt="cycle lane Diagram" src="../../diagrams/cycle lane.dot.png" /> <!-- Fallback for non-SVG browsers -->
</object>

Clause: 3.3.3.1

Note 1 to entry: Cycle lanes are typically much narrower than [traffic lanes](traffic lane.md).

Note 2 to entry: Cycle lanes are typically part of either a [carriageway](carriageway.md) or a [cycleway](cycleway.md).

Note 3 to entry: Cycle lanes could also allow and be designed for [motorized vehicles](motorized vehicle.md) (e.g. e-scooters, mopeds, etc.) with similar performance characteristics as human-powered cycles, subject to local regulations.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
