# jurisdictional entity

[person](person.md) or organization who has [jurisdiction](jurisdiction.md)

<object type="image/svg+xml" data="../../diagrams/jurisdictional entity.dot.svg">
    <img alt="jurisdictional entity Diagram" src="../../diagrams/jurisdictional entity.dot.png" /> <!-- Fallback for non-SVG browsers -->
</object>

Clause: 3.4.3.7

EXAMPLE: A city government

Note 1 to entry: A jurisdictional entity assigns its authority to one or more rule-makers, which may be itself (e.g., the individual owner or a campus) or other divisions of the entity (e.g., police department, traffic department).

Note 2 to entry: In the case of a geopolitical jurisdiction, the jurisdictional entity is typically the recognized government for the territory; in the case of a campus, the jurisdictional entity is typically the landowner.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2025
