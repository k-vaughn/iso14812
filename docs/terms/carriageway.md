# carriageway

contiguous area of paved [roadway](roadway.md) designed for the use of vehicles along a [road segment](road segment.md)

</object>

Clause: 3.3.1.5

Note 1 to entry: A carriageway is comprised of one or more [traffic lanes](traffic lane.md) [i.e. the [usable width](usable width.md)] and could include [shoulders](shoulder.md) and [lay-bys](lay-by.md).

Note 2 to entry: Carriageways are separated by [physical traffic separators](physical traffic separator.md).

Note 3 to entry: When looking at a [cross-section](cross-section.md) of the [road reservation](road reservation.md), the roadway consists of all of the carriageways.

History note: 2025: Revised to add "paved" and "designed for the use of vehicles".

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
