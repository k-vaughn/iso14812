# road segment

[link](link.md) that represents a contiguous length of a [road link](road link.md) characterized by the same physical characteristics

<object type="image/svg+xml" data="../../diagrams/road segment.dot.svg">
    <img alt="road segment Diagram" src="../../diagrams/road segment.dot.png" /> <!-- Fallback for non-SVG browsers -->
</object>

Clause: 3.3.5.8

Note 1 to entry: The definition of road segments is highly dependent on which characteristics are modelled by the implementation. Characteristics that can result in a new road segment include addition or subtraction of a [lane](lane.md), a change in [roadway](roadway.md) width, the change of [road](road.md) type (e.g. start/end of a bridge), etc.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
