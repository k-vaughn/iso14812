# Jersey barrier

[physical traffic separator](physical traffic separator.md) that consists of a wall with a wide, sloped base designed to divert [vehicles](vehicle.md) back into their [traffic lane](traffic lane.md)

Clause: 3.3.2.3

Admitted term: city block

Admitted term: concrete barrier

Note 1 to entry: Jersey barriers are typically modular. They are typically made of concrete but can also be made with other materials such as plastic filled with water or sand.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
