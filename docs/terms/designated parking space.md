Note 1 to entry: Designated parking spaces include those that require permits (e.g., reserved for those with disabilities) as well as advisory notices that rely upon voluntary compliance (e.g., reserved for expectant mothers).

Note 2 to entry: The term "designated parking space" is often specialized to indicate the type of designation (e.g., "electric vehicle parking space").

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2025
