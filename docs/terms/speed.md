# speed

rate of change of a [material entity's](material entity.md) position with respect to a frame of reference

<object type="image/svg+xml" data="../../diagrams/speed.dot.svg">
    <img alt="speed Diagram" src="../../diagrams/speed.dot.png" /> <!-- Fallback for non-SVG browsers -->
</object>

Clause: 3.7.2.1

Note 1 to entry: Speed is a function of time.

Note 2 to entry: Speed is the scalar absolute value (magnitude) of velocity.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022

## Specializations of speed

| Class | Description |
| --- | --- |
| [high vehicle speed](high vehicle speed.md) | [speed](speed.md) of a [vehicle](vehicle.md) that exceeds free flow motorway speeds by a factor of two or more |
| [low vehicle speed](low vehicle speed.md) | [speed](speed.md) of a [vehicle](vehicle.md) that exceeds [pedestrian](pedestrian.md) speeds and is more typical of pedal cycles |
| [moderate vehicle speed](moderate vehicle speed.md) | [speed](speed.md) of a [vehicle](vehicle.md) that exceeds [moderately-low vehicle speeds](moderately-low vehicle speed.md) but does not reach free-flow motorway speeds |
| [moderately-high vehicle speed](moderately-high vehicle speed.md) | [speed](speed.md) of a [vehicle](vehicle.md) that is typical of free-flow motorway speeds to twice that speed |
| [moderately-low vehicle speed](moderately-low vehicle speed.md) | [speed](speed.md) of a [vehicle](vehicle.md) that exceeds speeds typical of pedal cycles but can be reached on performance pedal cycles in short bursts or by performance riders |
| [ultra-low vehicle speed](ultra-low vehicle speed.md) | [speed](speed.md) of a [vehicle](vehicle.md) that does not greatly exceed typical [pedestrian](pedestrian.md) speeds |
