# interchange

[junction](junction.md) with at least one [grade separated manoeuvre](grade separated manoeuvre.md)

</object>

Clause: 3.3.6.9

Note 1 to entry: The grade separation allows [travellers](traveller.md) on the [link](link.md) to pass unimpeded through the interchange when congestion is not present.

Note 2 to entry: The term "interchange" typically refers to the facilities that enable all available manoeuvres at the grade separated [intersection](intersection.md), which typically includes multiple junctions.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
