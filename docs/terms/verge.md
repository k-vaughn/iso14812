# verge

unpaved part of the [roadside](roadside.md) designed for travel safety purposes

<object type="image/svg+xml" data="../../diagrams/verge.dot.svg">
    <img alt="verge Diagram" src="../../diagrams/verge.dot.png" /> <!-- Fallback for non-SVG browsers -->
</object>

Clause: 3.3.1.11

Note 1 to entry: A verge can separate two [lanes](lane.md) of different modes, separate a [traffic lane](traffic lane.md) from a ditch, or separate a lane from another safety hazard.

Note 2 to entry: Roadside, [shoulder](shoulder.md) and verge define similar but slightly different concepts.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
