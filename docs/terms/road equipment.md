# road equipment

component of [transport infrastructure](transport infrastructure.md) along the roadway intended to contribute to the safety, operations, management, awareness, and comfort of movements of the [road users](road user.md)

</object>

Clause: 3.3.9.1

Admitted term: road furniture

EXAMPLE: sign, traffic signal, safety rail, gantry, bus stop structure, highway safety lighting

Note 1 to entry: ITS roadside equipment is a type of roadside equipment.

Note 2 to entry: Road equipment can be next to, over, on and/or in the roadway.

Note 3 to entry: Road equipment is sometimes referred to as "roadside equipment".

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
