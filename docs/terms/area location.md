# area location

[spatial location](spatial location.md) enclosed within a two-dimensional boundary or boundaries across a defined surface

<object type="image/svg+xml" data="../../diagrams/area location.dot.svg">
    <img alt="area location Diagram" src="../../diagrams/area location.dot.png" /> <!-- Fallback for non-SVG browsers -->
</object>

Clause: 3.4.1.6

Alternative preferred term: ITS area location

EXAMPLE: The British Isles.

Note 1 to entry: The boundary can consist of a single curvilinear line (e.g. a circle) or multiple curvilinear lines (e.g. the boundaries of city limits) and can consist of multiple enclosed areas.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
