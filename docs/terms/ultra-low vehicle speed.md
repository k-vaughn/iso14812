# ultra-low vehicle speed

[speed](speed.md) of a [vehicle](vehicle.md) that does not greatly exceed typical [pedestrian](pedestrian.md) speeds

Clause: 3.7.5.7

EXAMPLE: SAE J3194 associates this with a top speed of less than 13 km/h.

Note 1 to entry: Local regulations for the exact speed range vary.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
