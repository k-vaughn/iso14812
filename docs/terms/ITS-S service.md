# ITS-S service

communication functionality of an [ITS station](ITS station.md) that provides the capability to connect to other nodes

    <img alt="ITS-S service Diagram" src="../../diagrams/ITS-S service.dot.png" /> <!-- Fallback for non-SVG browsers -->
</object>

Clause: 3.5.4.5

Note 1 to entry: The definition of ITS-S service predates the adoption of the concept model used for the other service terms in this standard. It is an outlier in that it describes a portion of the functionality contained within the ITS station reference architecture (i.e., the communication functionality) rather than the end functionality of the conceptual ITS station.

Note 2 to entry: [ISO 21217:2020, 3.51; note added]

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2025
