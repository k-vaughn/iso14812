# planning architecture

[architecture](architecture.md) that provides a long-term vision of system [elements](element.md) that may be deployed and managed by different projects and/or [entities](entity.md) within a geographic area

Clause: 3.1.9.2

Admitted term: regional architecture

Note 1 to entry: Some countries use the term "regional architecture", but in International Standards, the term "regional" is avoided due to its multiple meanings.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
