# lay-by

short length of paved [carriageway](carriageway.md) at its edge designed to allow a [vehicle](vehicle.md) to draw out of the [traffic lanes](traffic lane.md) and stop temporarily

</object>

Clause: 3.3.1.16

Note 1 to entry: Lay-bys are not part of a [hard shoulder](hard shoulder.md).

Note 2 to entry: Lay-bys can be used for emergency situations, picking up/dropping off [passengers](passenger.md), etc.

History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
