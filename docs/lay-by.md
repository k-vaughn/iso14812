# lay-by

short length of paved carriageway (3.3.1.5) at its edge designed to allow a
            vehicle (3.7.1.1) to draw out of the traffic lanes (3.3.1.13) and stop temporarily

NOTE: Lay-bys are not part of a hard shoulder (3.3.1.19).
