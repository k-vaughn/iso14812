# ultra-low-speed vehicle

vehicle (3.7.1.1) with a design speed (3.7.2.2) that does not exceed
            ultra-low vehicle speeds (3.7.5.7)

NOTE: The term "pedestrian speed vehicle" is allowed but the preferred
            term follows a logical progression of levels.
