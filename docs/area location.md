# area location

spatial location (3.4.1.2) enclosed within a two-dimensional boundary or
            boundaries across a defined surface

NOTE: The boundary can consist of a single curvilinear line (e.g. a
            circle) or multiple curvilinear lines (e.g. the boundaries of city limits) and can
            consist of multiple enclosed areas.

EXAMPLE: The British Isles.
