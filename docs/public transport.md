# public transport

transport service (3.5.2.1) that is publicly accessible enabling the
            movement of one or more persons (3.1.1.6)

NOTE: A public transport service can be scheduled or on-demand.

EXAMPLE: 1 Bus, tram, cable car, metro, train and ferry are examples of public
            transport means for collective use.
