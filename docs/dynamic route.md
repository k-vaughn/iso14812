# dynamic route

<transport service> concurrent (3.5.9.3) operation where transported
            items can only be received or delivered at stopping points within a pre-defined service
            (3.5.1.1) corridor

NOTE: The service corridor is defined by the provider, who may impose
            further restrictions on where stops are allowed.

EXAMPLE: Airport shuttle.
