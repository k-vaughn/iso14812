# free-floating

<transport service> network model (3.5.10.1) where the transport
            service (3.5.2.1) may be initiated and terminated at any location (3.4.1.1) meeting
            basic criteria

NOTE: The basic criteria typically include geographic limits and
            requirements related to legal and safe locations.
