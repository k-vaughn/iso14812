    cycleway¶

    infrastructure primarily designed for the use of cycles and separate from a carriageway

    Clause: 3.3.3.2

    Alternative preferred term: cycle track

    Note 1 to entry: Local legislation can allow cycleways to be used by a variety of moderately low-speed vehicles (e.g. low-speed scooters, skateboards, etc.) and can share usage of other low-speed modes such as pedestrians, horseback riders, etc.

    History note: Introduced in ISO/TS 14812:2022
